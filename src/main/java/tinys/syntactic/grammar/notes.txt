grammar4.txt:
    - λ productions are always present in Opt, List and Tail ending rules
    - Operand ::= Literal | Primary ChainingOpt is ambiguous, as all Primary derivations can be followed by ChainingOpt => Operand ::= Literal | Primary
    here, it is being allowed only one level of postfix/chaining
    Same goes for StaticMethodCall and MethodCall
